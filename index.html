What

This is your go-to resource on designing ethically. This framework lays down the theoretical and philosophical foundation and also provides a toolkit for you to implement the ideas presented here.

Who

This ethical design framework originated from a team of designers, product managers, consultants, and engineers from various tech companies who agreed that enough was enough.

When

Now. We need designers in every step of their career to push for more ethical decision-making in their companies. There's already been extensive damage done, but it's not too late to work on reversing the current consequences.

How

Aside from paying this knowledge forward, we encourage you to contribute to this framework. This is by no means set in stone - like our design process, this framework is meant to evolve and iterate. Your thoughts are welcome.
